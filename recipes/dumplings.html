Dumplings Recipe

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 cup warm water

Filling

  • 2 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup finely chopped napa cabbage
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Dipping Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon chili oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and warm water to form a dough. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine pork, cabbage, green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll the dough into thin circles. Place a teaspoon of filling in the center. Fold and seal.
  4. Steam for 10 minutes, boil until they float, or pan-fry until golden brown.
  5. Serve with dipping sauce.

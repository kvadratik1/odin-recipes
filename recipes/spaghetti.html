Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta with garlic until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta and add it to the pancetta pan (off heat).
  5. Slowly stir in the egg mixture, tossing quickly to coat the pasta.
  6. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time for a creamy texture.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper. Serve hot with extra Parmesan.
